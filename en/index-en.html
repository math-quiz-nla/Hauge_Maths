Introduction to Algebra Knowledge Test

Welcome to the page where you can test your algebra knowledge. The purpose of this test is to help you assess whether you have the necessary background knowledge required for courses such as mathematics for economics. If the test results show low skill levels, a preparatory course in mathematics is recommended before taking such courses.

The test consists of 11 categories, each with five multiple-choice questions. You can start the test by selecting any category. Answer all the questions and save your answers. Use the navigation buttons to move between categories. Unanswered questions will be marked as incorrect in the final score.

At the end of the test, a report will be generated showing your total score out of 55 and your skill level in each category. The results will be presented as a radar chart or a bar chart. Links to Khan Academy materials for each category will be provided to help you improve your skills.
